Data Deletion Instructions for IT-helpdesk

To delete your data from IT-helpdesk, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an Email: Send an email to jarudat.jc@gmail.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request."
  2. Include Information: In the email, please include the following information:
    • Your full name
    • Your email address
    • The Facebook ID linked to your account
  3. Processing Time: Our support team will process your request, and all of your data will be deleted from our system within 7 days.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at jarudat.jc@gmail.com.
